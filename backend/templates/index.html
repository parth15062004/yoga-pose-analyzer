About Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

Surya Namaskar, or Sun Salutation, is a sequence of 12 powerful yoga poses. It is a complete body workout that revitalizes the body, mind, and spirit. Practicing all 12 poses in sequence helps improve flexibility, strength, and balance, and is a great way to start your day with energy and mindfulness.

1. Pranamasana
(प्रणामासन)
Prayer Pose: Stand at the front of your mat, join your palms together, and center yourself.
2. Hasta Uttanasana
(हस्त उत्तानासन)
Raised Arms Pose: Inhale and lift your arms up and back, keeping the biceps close to the ears.
3. Padahastasana
(पादहस्तासन)
Hand to Foot Pose: Exhale and bend forward from the waist, keeping the spine erect.
4. Ashwa Sanchalanasana
(अश्व संचालनासन)
Equestrian Pose: Inhale and stretch your right leg back, keeping the left foot between the hands.
5. Dandasana
(दण्डासन)
Stick Pose: Exhale and take the left leg back, bringing the body in a straight line.
6. Ashtanga Namaskara
(अष्टांग नमस्कार)
Salute with Eight Parts: Gently touch your knees, chest, and chin to the floor.
7. Bhujangasana
(भुजंगासन)
Cobra Pose: Slide forward and raise the chest up into the cobra posture.
8. Adho Mukha Svanasana
(अधोमुख श्वानासन)
Downward Dog Pose: Exhale and lift the hips, forming an inverted V shape.
9. Ashwa Sanchalanasana
(अश्व संचालनासन)
Equestrian Pose (Other Side): Inhale and bring the right foot forward between the hands.
10. Padahastasana
(पादहस्तासन)
Hand to Foot Pose: Exhale and bring the left foot forward, keeping the palms on the floor.
11. Hasta Uttanasana
(हस्त उत्तानासन)
Raised Arms Pose: Inhale and lift your arms up and back.
12. Pranamasana
(प्रणामासन)
Prayer Pose: Exhale and gently bring your palms together at the chest.
